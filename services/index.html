How We Work Together

Step 1

Discovery Call (30 min, free)

We discuss your challenges, data landscape, and business objectives. I'll ask questions to understand constraints and success metrics. No sales pitch—just a technical conversation.

Step 2

Proposal & Timeline

I provide a detailed proposal with scope, deliverables, timeline, and cost. Includes example outputs and success criteria. Typical turnaround: 3-5 business days.

Step 3

Kickoff & Data Access

We set up secure data transfer (S3, SFTP, API keys), align on stakeholders, and establish communication cadence. I do initial data profiling to catch issues early.

Step 4

Build & Weekly Check-ins

I work in 1-week sprints with Friday demos. You see progress incrementally and can course-correct early. Code is on GitHub; documentation is live in Notion/Google Docs.

Step 5

Deployment & Handoff

Final testing, stakeholder training, and deployment. I provide runbooks, troubleshooting guides, and 30 days of post-launch support. Optional: monthly retainer for ongoing improvements.
